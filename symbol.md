## 函数

$$
\mathrm{sign}(x) =
\begin{cases}
x > 0 & 1 \\
x < 0 & 0
\end{cases}
$$

$$
\mathrm{I}(\text{condition}) =
\begin{cases}
1 & \text{condition = True} \\
0 & \text{condition = False}
\end{cases}
$$
